An internet usage policy dictates what is deemed to be appropriate internet browsing behaviour in the workplace. This policy typically enforces restrictions for employees when browsing the internet for streaming media. Accessing YouTube and Torrents are strictly prohibited at office. IT department uses analyzer tools to monitor internet usage by IP Address. All the employees are suggested to update their machine names with their actual names. Having an internet usage policy, AIS ensures that employees are following directives that serve to safeguard their work environment and the IT network infrastructure. For more details regarding this policy you can contact Srikanth.goud@appliedis.com
