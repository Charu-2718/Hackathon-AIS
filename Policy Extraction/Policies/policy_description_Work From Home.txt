The employer recognizes that there may, on occasion, be circumstances when it would be more beneficial or flexible for staff to work at home, either on a permanent basis, or in order to complete a particular task. WFH is highly valued as an effective work life balance. However, it needs to be used in the right spirit. Following are some criteria & guidelines to bring consistency:1.       Establishing a good working relationship with the manager & team2.       Valid reason to work remotely3.       Ownership lies with the employee to eliminate any impact to workHere are some guidelines to ensure this is effective.1.       Make an entry in ERMS & get approval from the manager2.       Ensure infrastructure can support an uninterrupted work day - power backup, internet connection...3.       Be available on Lync/Skype4.       Notify team members of your timings/breaks during WFH.
